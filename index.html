Mayo & Connacht Region, Ireland

Welcome!

This directory has been created to help new home educating families in Mayo and the wider Connacht region find local support groups, community connections, and educational activities for their children. The directory includes: • Home Education Groups & Support - National and regional organisations • Home Ed Meetups - Local activities specifically for home ed families (community recommended!) • Activities by County - Educational venues, nature centers, museums, and family activities Home education is legal in Ireland and enshrined in the Irish constitution. Tusla maintains a register of home educated children. Last Updated: February 2026 Includes recommendations from local home ed community
