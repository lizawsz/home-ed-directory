## Objection Handling

### "We already have good visibility"

**Response:**

That's great! The home education community is a bit different though - they often find activities through specialized directories and Facebook groups rather than general search.

Our directory is specifically shared in 10+ home ed Facebook groups with thousands of members. Many families tell us they found venues they didn't know existed, even ones they drive past regularly.

Think of it as targeted marketing to a niche audience that's actively looking for exactly what you offer, during off-peak times when you might have capacity.

### "That's expensive for a listing"

**Response:**

I completely understand. Let me break down the value:

- €50/month = €1.67 per day
- If we send you just 2 additional families per month, and each spends €30-50, you've more than covered the cost
- Plus, home ed families tend to be repeat visitors and bring friends

Would the €25/month Basic Partner option work better for you? You still get highlighted placement and extended description - great for testing if the home ed market works for your venue.

### "We don't have budget for marketing right now"

**Response:**

No problem! Your free listing will stay active in the directory.

If you'd like to explore this in the future, just reach out. Many of our partners started with the free listing and upgraded once they saw the home ed community's interest in their venue.

In the meantime, would you be willing to link to the directory from your website? That helps families discover us both.

### "How do we know it will work?"

**Response:**

Great question. Here's what I propose:

**Trial Offer:** Try the Basic Partner tier (€25/month) for 3 months at 50% off (€37.50 total). I'll provide monthly reports showing:
- How many people clicked your listing
- Which search terms found you
- Email newsletter engagement

If you're not seeing value, cancel anytime. No long-term commitment.

Plus, I'll create a unique discount code for your venue (like "HOMEED10") so you can track exactly which bookings came from the directory.

Sound fair?

### "We already offer home ed discounts"

**Response:**

That's fantastic! That actually makes the partnership even more valuable.

We can prominently display your home ed discount in your listing, and promote it in our monthly newsletter. Many families specifically look for home-ed-friendly venues that understand their needs.

Your existing discount becomes a marketing advantage that we'll help get in front of hundreds of families who may not know about it yet.

## Tips for Success

### Before You Pitch
1. **Visit the venue** (if possible) - Personal connection helps
2. **Research their offerings** - Tailor pitch to their strengths
3. **Check their current marketing** - Understand their approach
4. **Identify decision-maker** - Manager/owner, not front desk
5. **Have mockups ready** - Show what their listing will look like

### During the Pitch
1. **Lead with value to them** - Not what you need, what they gain
2. **Use numbers** - "1,200 families" sounds better than "many families"
3. **Share success stories** - Social proof matters
4. **Make it low-risk** - Trial periods, monthly vs. annual
5. **Ask for the sale** - "Would you like to start this month?"

### After the Sale
1. **Deliver quickly** - Get their featured listing live within days
2. **Over-communicate** - Monthly reports, updates, wins
3. **Be responsive** - Answer questions fast
4. **Show results** - Track and share their performance
5. **Ask for referrals** - "Know other venues that might benefit?"

---

**Remember:** You're not selling advertising - you're offering a targeted way to reach a specific, valuable customer segment during off-peak times. Frame it as a partnership, not a transaction.
