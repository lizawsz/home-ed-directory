## 📊 Business Model Summary

### Revenue Streams
1. **Sponsored Listings** - €25-50/month per venue
   - Target: 30+ potential sponsors identified
   - Featured placement, enhanced visibility

2. **Premium Memberships** - €5/month or €50/year for families
   - Benefits: Newsletter, printables, exclusive discounts, private community

3. **Google AdSense** - Passive ad revenue

4. **Affiliate Commissions** - Heritage cards, curriculum providers

5. **Event Promotions** - €10-50 per event feature

### Target Market
- **Primary:** 800-1,200 home educating families in Connacht
- **Expansion:** All of Ireland (~8,000-10,000 families)
- **Growth:** 20%+ year-on-year in Ireland

### Key Success Factors
1. Community trust (free value first)
2. Mobile-first experience
3. Regular content updates
4. Authentic engagement in home ed groups
5. Quality over quantity

## 💡 Key Insights from Discussion

### Domain Naming
- Don't limit to "Mayo & Connacht" - think Ireland-wide
- Short, memorable domains are worth it (homeed.ie)
- Future-proof with subdomain strategy (mayo.homeed.ie)

### Launch Strategy
- Both website and Google Sheets have merits
- Google Sheets = lower barrier, easier tracking initially
- Website = more professional, better for monetization later
- Hybrid approach possible

### Monetization
- Don't monetize immediately - build trust first
- Multiple revenue streams = more resilient
- Venues will pay for access to niche market (off-peak times)
- €15/year domain cost is worth it for a €5,500+ business

### Community Approach
- Serve families first, revenue second
- Free version must stay valuable
- Transparency about sponsored content
- Give back to community
