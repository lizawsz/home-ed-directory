# Distribution Strategy

How to get the Mayo & Connacht Home Education Directory in front of home educating families.

## 🎯 Target Audience

**Primary:** Home educating families in Mayo, Galway, Sligo, Roscommon, and Leitrim

**Estimated Market Size:**
- Ireland: ~8,000-10,000 home educated children (2024)
- Connacht region: ~800-1,200 families (estimate 10% of national)
- Mayo alone: ~200-300 families (estimate)

**Secondary Audience:**
- Families considering home education
- Activity providers wanting to reach home ed market
- Educational resource providers

## ⚠️ Important Considerations

**Privacy:**
- Never spam groups
- Follow group rules (some don't allow links)
- Ask admin permission first in smaller groups
- Don't over-post (once is enough, update quarterly)

**Authenticity:**
- Be genuinely helpful, not salesy
- Engage in discussions, don't just drop links
- Share your own home ed story if appropriate
- Build relationships in community first

**Sustainability:**
- Aim for organic growth
- Quality over quantity
- Better 500 engaged users than 5,000 visitors who never return
- Focus on being genuinely useful
